# 🥥 Coconut Clicker Game

A fun and addictive coconut clicking idle game where you click to earn coconuts and upgrade your clicking power!

## 🎮 Play Now

**[Click here to play the game!](https://cosmlctv.github.io/clicker/)**

## ✨ Game Features

- 🥥 **Click the Coconut** - Each click earns you coconuts
- 💪 **Upgrade System** - Increase your coconuts per click
- 💾 **Save/Load Progress** - Never lose your progress
- 📊 **Statistics** - Track your total clicks
- 🎨 **Beautiful Animations** - Floating numbers and visual effects
- 💫 **Auto-Save** - Game saves automatically every 30 seconds

## 🎯 How to Play

1. **Click the big coconut** 🥥 to earn coconuts
2. **Buy upgrades** 💪 to earn more coconuts per click
3. **Save your progress** 💾 to continue later
4. **Keep clicking** to become the ultimate coconut tycoon!
## 📱 Mobile Friendly

The game works great on both desktop and mobile devices! Tap the coconut on your phone to play anywhere.
